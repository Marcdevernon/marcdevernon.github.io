Research experience
======
* **Graduate Research Assistant**, Southern Methodist University, Dallas, TX, 2022&ndash;present
  * Advisor: Prof. Thomas Hagstrom. Discontinuous Galerkin methods for multi-scale, second-order linear wave equations in periodic media over long time horizons.
  * Improving DG schemes for multi-scale interface problems with variable wave speeds, reducing compute cost while preserving fine-structure resolution.
  * Applying mathematical homogenization to capture sub-grid behavior in heterogeneous media without explicit fine-mesh resolution.
  * Reduced-order modeling of damping layers using rank-adaptive time stepping.
* **O'Donnell Data Science Fellow**, O'Donnell Institute, SMU, Dallas, TX, Aug 2024&ndash;May 2025
  * Algorithms for reduced-order modeling and data assimilation, with applications to digital twins of complex flow systems.
  * Studied the perturbed sine-Gordon equation and characterized its multi-scale, embedded chaotic dynamics across scales.
  * Developed preliminary error-bound analysis for Galerkin time evolution of POD coefficients.
* **Visiting Research Intern**, U.S. Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL), Edwards AFB, CA, Summer 2024
  * Hosted by Dr. Hagstrom and Dr. Munipalli. Interfacial boundary conditions for coupling reduced-order models in multi-scale, multi-physics rocket-propulsion problems.
  * Demonstrated that flux conditions on domain-decomposition interfaces do not increase error in POD-based reduced-order models.
